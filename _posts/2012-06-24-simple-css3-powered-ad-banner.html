Ad Banners have one purpose only and thats to grab the attention of the readers and make them click through. With this in mind I designed a simple one with some simple CSS3 border-radius property.


Demo


To add this Ad Banner Widget into your blog, you can use the one click installer


Or you can add it manually

To do that just add the following CSS and HTML to a HTML/JavaScript widget
